MANALI

Where Mountains Meet Serenity

Solang Valley

A paradise for adventure lovers, Solang Valley offers activities like paragliding, skiing, and snowboarding along with breathtaking mountain views.

Rohtang Pass

Located at a high altitude, Rohtang Pass is known for its snow-covered landscapes and stunning views, making it a must-visit destination near Manali.

Hadimba Temple

Surrounded by cedar forests, this ancient temple is known for its unique wooden architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

Old Manali

Known for its cozy cafes, scenic views, and relaxed vibe, Old Manali is perfect for travelers looking to unwind and explore local culture.
