AMRITSAR

The Spiritual Heart of Punjab

Golden Temple

The holiest shrine of Sikhism, the Golden Temple is known for its serene atmosphere and stunning golden architecture. The langar (community kitchen) serves free meals to thousands every day.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historic garden and memorial that stands as a reminder of the tragic 1919 massacre during British rule. It is a place of remembrance and national importance.

Wagah Border

Famous for the daily Beating Retreat ceremony, Wagah Border showcases patriotic energy and synchronized military drills between India and Pakistan.

Durgiana Temple

Often compared to the Golden Temple in architecture, this Hindu temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga and offers a peaceful spiritual experience.
