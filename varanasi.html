VARANASI

The Eternal City of India

Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the most vibrant ghats of Varanasi, famous for the grand Ganga Aarti held every evening with lamps, chants, and spiritual energy.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, attracting millions of devotees every year. It stands as a symbol of faith and devotion.

Assi Ghat

A peaceful spot where the Ganga meets the Assi river, popular for sunrise views, yoga sessions, and a calm atmosphere.

Banaras Streets

The narrow गलियाँ of Varanasi are full of life, temples, local shops, and authentic street food, offering a true glimpse of ancient India.
