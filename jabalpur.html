JABALPUR

City of Marble Rocks

Kachnaar City Temple

Kachnar City Temple is one of the most famous landmarks in Jabalpur, known for its 76-foot tall statue of Lord Shiva. The temple offers a peaceful spiritual atmosphere surrounded by greenery and is a popular spot for both tourists and devotees.

Dhuandhar Fall

Located on the Narmada River, Dhuandhar Falls is a natural wonder where the river plunges down from a considerable height, creating a smoky mist-like effect ("Dhuan" means smoke). The falls are surrounded by lush greenery and offer breathtaking views.

Bargi Dam

Located about 40 kilometers from Jabalpur, Bargi Dam is a major reservoir on the Narmada River. The dam offers boating facilities, and the surrounding area is ideal for picnics and birdwatching.

Marble Rocks

These marble cliffs along the Narmada River are a sight to behold, especially during a boat ride. The river flowing between these tall, white rocks creates a mesmerizing landscape, particularly when illuminated by the sunlight.
